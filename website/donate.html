Donate

Every rand plants something that lasts

Safe Cities is a registered non-profit in Elsies River, Cape Town. Donations go directly into the farm and our training programmes. There are no shareholders, only neighbours.

On the farm

What your donation grows

  • Food gardens

    Seedlings, compost and irrigation for permaculture beds that put fresh vegetables on family tables every week.

  • Tools & training

    Hand tools, building materials and course costs for construction and permaculture skills training.

  • Young leaders

    Mentorship, workshops and theatre training that give young people confidence and a voice in their community.
